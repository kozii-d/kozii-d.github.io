Key Features

Instant Hover Translation

Just hover over a single word in the subtitles, and the translation will appear immediately.

Phrase & Sentence Translation

Select multiple words (holding Shift) to translate entire phrases or sentences.

Supports 190+ Languages

Translate from virtually any language to your native language with ease.

Auto Language Detection

HoverTranslate automatically detects the language of the subtitles.

Auto-Pause Feature

Videos automatically pause when you hover over subtitles, ensuring you never miss a single word.

Works with Auto-Generated Subtitles

Even auto-generated subtitles on YouTube can be translated with a simple hover.

How It Works

1. Install HoverTranslate from the Chrome Web Store.
2. Enable the extension and sign in with Google.
3. Play a YouTube video with subtitles.
4. Hover your mouse over any word or select multiple words to see an instant translation.
5. You can manage the extension’s settings or sign out at any time.
